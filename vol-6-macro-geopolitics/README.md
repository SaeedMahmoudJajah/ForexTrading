# 🌍 The Forex Trading Playbook — Volume 6

## Fundamental & Macro Analysis — Geopolitics, Global Indicators & How to Study the Market

Technical analysis (Vol 3) tells you *where* and *when*. **Macro tells you *why* and *which way*** — and it's the layer that separates competent chart traders from the top 1%. The traders who consistently extract fortunes from currencies — Soros, Druckenmiller (Vol 2) — are **global macro** thinkers first. They don't predict the next candle; they understand where the world's money is flowing and why, then position ahead of it.

This volume teaches you to **read the world like a macro trader**: how central banks and interest rates drive currencies, which economic indicators actually matter (and how), how geopolitics and risk sentiment move money across borders, and — most importantly — **how to build a research process** that turns all of it into a clear directional bias you can trade.

> ⚠️ **The honest framing.** Macro will *not* give you a crystal ball. Currencies are driven by the *relative* outlook of two economies, filtered through what the market has **already priced in** — so being "right" about the economy and still losing money is common. Macro's real power is to give you a **high-conviction directional bias and context**, which you then express with the timing of technicals (Vol 3) and the risk discipline of the rest of the series. Macro without risk management is just sophisticated gambling.

This is **Volume 6**. It deepens the fundamentals introduced in **[Vol 1](../README.md)**, thinks like the macro legends of **[Vol 2](../vol-2-top-traders/README.md)**, pairs with the technicals of **[Vol 3](../vol-3-technical-analysis/README.md)**, and feeds the systems of **[Vol 4](../vol-4-strategy-systems/README.md)**.

## The macro edge in one diagram

```
   GEOPOLITICS & GLOBAL EVENTS                INTERMARKET (bonds, stocks, commodities)
            │                                            │
            ▼                                            ▼
   ┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
   │  CENTRAL BANKS & INTEREST-RATE EXPECTATIONS  (the engine)     │
   └─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
            │                  driven by ▲ economic data
            ▼                            │
   RELATIVE OUTLOOK of Currency A vs Currency B
            │
            ▼   filtered through what's ALREADY PRICED IN (expectations)
   YOUR DIRECTIONAL BIAS  →  timed with TECHNICALS (Vol 3)  →  risk-managed (Vols 1,4)
```

> 🔑 **The top-1% question is never "is this economy good?"** It's *"is this economy's outlook better or worse than the OTHER currency's, relative to what the market has already priced?"* Master that relative, expectations-adjusted way of thinking and you see currencies the way the best traders do.
