# 🗺️ The Forex Trading Playbook — Volume 8

## Market Deep Dive — Currency & Pair Profiles

Every currency has a **personality.** EUR/USD grinds in orderly ranges; GBP/JPY lunges like a wild animal; USD/JPY moves to the heartbeat of US bond yields; the Aussie dances with Chinese growth. The top traders don't trade "forex" generically — they trade *specific instruments they know intimately*, the way a sailor knows particular waters.

This volume is the field guide. It profiles each major currency and pair in depth: **what drives it, how it behaves, when it's most active, what it correlates with, and how to trade it.** It turns the macro framework of Volume 6 into concrete, instrument-by-instrument knowledge you can act on.

> ⚠️ **Profiles describe tendencies, not laws.** A currency's "personality" — its typical volatility, correlations, and drivers — is a *strong tendency* that shifts with the regime (Vol 6). The yen's link to US yields, the Aussie's link to risk sentiment, gold's inverse to the dollar — these are reliable *most* of the time and break in unusual conditions. Use these profiles as a deep starting map, always re-checked against the *current* macro environment, never as fixed rules.

This is **Volume 8**. It applies the macro drivers of **[Vol 6](../vol-6-macro-geopolitics/README.md)**, the session/pair basics of **[Vol 1](../README.md)**, and feeds the focused market-selection of your plan in **[Vol 7](../vol-7-complete-trading-plan/README.md)**.

## The major currencies at a glance

```
RESERVE / CENTER:   USD 🇺🇸  — on one side of ~88% of all FX trades; the gravity well
MAJOR PARTNERS:     EUR 🇪🇺  GBP 🇬🇧                (the European majors)
SAFE HAVENS:        USD 🇺🇸  JPY 🇯🇵  CHF 🇨🇭        (+ gold) — bid in fear
COMMODITY DOLLARS:  AUD 🇦🇺  NZD 🇳🇿  CAD 🇨🇦        — bid in risk-on / commodity strength
                    (AUD↔metals/China, NZD↔dairy/risk, CAD↔oil)
```

> 🔑 **Currencies sort into roles: the reserve/center (USD), the European majors (EUR, GBP), the safe havens (USD, JPY, CHF), and the commodity dollars (AUD, NZD, CAD).** Knowing a currency's *role* tells you most of how it behaves in risk-on vs risk-off (Vol 6). The profiles in this volume fill in the rest.
