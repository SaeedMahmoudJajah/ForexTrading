# 🏛️ The Forex Trading Playbook — Volume 12

## The 10-Year Tycoon Roadmap — From Zero to a Trading Enterprise

Everyone who opens a trading account dreams of wealth. Almost none plan for it like the decade-long enterprise it actually is. "Tycoon" is not a weekend of lucky trades or a viral month — it is the compounding of *skill, capital, discipline, and business-building* over **ten years**. This volume is that plan: a phased, year-by-year roadmap that takes you from an absolute beginner with a day job to — if you do the work and the odds break your way — a wealthy, full-time operator running trading as a serious enterprise.

This is the **capstone of the entire series**. It doesn't replace the other volumes — it *sequences* them across a decade, so you know what to learn, when to risk, when to scale, and when to diversify. It turns a library of knowledge into a life plan.

> ⚠️ **The brutally honest framing this volume insists on.** Becoming a trading "tycoon" is *rare* — far rarer than the internet pretends. Most who try forex lose money and quit; most who stay never get truly wealthy from it. This volume will **not** promise you riches, and anyone who does is selling you something (see [Vol 1](../README.md) and [Vol 11](../vol-11-ai-agents/README.md)). What it offers is the *highest-probability path*: a realistic, survival-first, decade-long plan that builds genuine skill and wealth-building capacity whether or not you ever reach "tycoon." Follow it and the *worst* likely outcome is that you become a competent, disciplined trader with real savings and a business mind. The *best* outcome is the title you came for. Both are worth the work.

This is **Volume 12**. It assumes the foundations of **[Vols 1–7](../README.md)**, the routine of **[Vol 10](../vol-10-daily-routine/README.md)**, and the funded path of **[Vol 5](../vol-5-prop-firm-path/README.md)** — and arranges them into a ten-year arc with milestones, money-management math, and an enterprise endgame.

## The decade at a glance

```
 PHASE 1            PHASE 2             PHASE 3              ENTERPRISE
 APPRENTICE         PROFESSIONAL        CAPITAL ALLOCATOR    TYCOON
 Years 1–2          Years 2–4           Years 4–7            Years 7–10
 ─────────────      ─────────────       ──────────────       ─────────────
 Learn the craft │ Prove consistency │ Scale capital &     │ Trade OTHER
 Survive & stay  │ Win funded        │ income streams      │ capital, build
 Keep day-job    │ capital (Vol 5)   │ Diversify wealth    │ a firm/team,
 income          │ Go full-time      │ Run it as a         │ systems & tech
 Build the base  │                   │ business            │ Multiply yourself
 ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
   SKILL  ───────────────►  +  CAPITAL  ──────────►  +  BUSINESS  ──────►  WEALTH
              all compounding, every year, for ten years
```

> 🔑 **Wealth in trading = skill × capital × time, protected by survival.** You can be brilliant and broke (no capital), or well-funded and reckless (no survival). The tycoon is the one who builds *all* the inputs — a real edge, growing capital, business sense — and, above everything, *never blows up*, year after year, so the compounding is never reset to zero. This volume is the ten-year sequence for assembling every input without ever losing the game.
