# Appendix — The 10-Year Tycoon Plan & Milestones

[← Risks, Reality & Longevity](08-risks-reality-longevity.md) | [Volume 12 Home](README.md)

---

This is where the volume becomes *your* plan. Copy these templates, fill them in, and revisit them every quarter and every year. The numbers are deliberately left blank or illustrative — **set your own targets** based on your capital, income, and life. What matters is not the specific figures but that you have written, dated milestones to measure against. A decade-long ambition with no milestones is a daydream; with milestones, it's a plan.

> 🔑 **Targets turn a ten-year dream into a ten-year plan.** You can't manage what you don't measure, and you can't stay disciplined across a decade without checkpoints. Fill these in, keep them visible (Vol 10), and review them on schedule — so every quarter you know whether you're on track, and every year you decide, on evidence, whether to advance a phase. The plan you write is worth more than the volume you read.

---

## A. The decade on one page

```
PHASE 0  PREP        Months 0–6    Decide · learn basics · write a plan · demo. Risk ~$0.
PHASE 1  APPRENTICE  Years 1–2     Skill & survival. Micro live. Keep day-job income.
PHASE 2  PROFESSIONAL Years 2–4    Consistency → funded capital (Vol 5) → full-time.
PHASE 3  ALLOCATOR   Years 4–7     Scale capital · diversify income & wealth · business.
PHASE 4  ENTERPRISE  Years 7–10    Others' capital · firm/team · systematize · multiply.

  Across all years: COMPOUND the engine, HARVEST into diversified wealth, SURVIVE.
```

## C. The phase-gate checklist (don't advance until met)

```
PHASE 1 → 2:  □ written plan followed  □ 6+ mo break-even+ live  □ documented edge
              □ discipline & emotional control  □ no blow-up  □ buffer forming

PHASE 2 → 3:  □ reliable multi-account income  □ prop firms re-funding you
              □ full-time (or deliberate side)  □ cash buffer + first diversified savings
              □ discipline intact AT SCALE  □ business mindset emerging

PHASE 3 → 4:  □ scaled capital, disciplined  □ multiple income streams
              □ structured business (entity/tax/accounting)  □ net worth mostly OUTSIDE trading
              □ teachable, documented system  □ credibility to manage external capital
```

## D. The annual review (do every year — Vol 10 cadence)

```
THE BUILDER'S FOUR QUESTIONS (Ch. 2) — answer honestly each year:
  1. Did my SKILL improve?        (KPIs better? fewer mistakes? — Vol 4)        Y / N: ___
  2. Did my CAPITAL grow?         (profits + savings + allocations)             Y / N: ___
  3. Did my BUSINESS advance?     (track record, structure, streams)            Y / N: ___
  4. Did I SURVIVE?               (no blow-up; within drawdown limits)          Y / N: ___

  All four YES → on the tycoon track regardless of this year's size.
  Any NO → that's next year's #1 focus. Fix the input, trust the output.

  This year's biggest win: ______________________________________
  This year's biggest mistake/lesson: ___________________________
  One change for next year: _____________________________________
  Advance a phase? (gate met?)  YES / NO / NOT YET
```

## F. The non-negotiables (tape these up)

```
  1. NEVER blow up. Survival is the divisor that protects everything (Ch. 1, 6).
  2. Risk a small fixed % per trade — discipline TIGHTENS as capital grows, never loosens.
  3. Don't skip a gate. Feeling ready ≠ being ready. Meet the standard, then advance.
  4. Compound early, harvest deliberately. Move wealth OFF the table over time.
  5. Keep most net worth OUTSIDE any single trading account.
  6. Protect the trader — health, balance, longevity. YOU are the key asset (Ch. 8).
  7. No shortcuts, no scams, no guaranteed-return schemes — ever (Vol 1, 11).
  8. Judge each year by the builder's four questions, not the bank balance.
```

```
Learn & survive → prove an edge → win funded capital & go pro → scale & diversify →
build the wealth base → multiply yourself into an enterprise — compounding skill, capital,
and reputation every year for ten years, and never, ever blowing up. That is the path.
```
