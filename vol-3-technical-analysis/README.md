# 📊 The Forex Trading Playbook — Volume 3

## Technical Analysis & Chart Mastery

Price is the only thing that pays you. This volume is a **deep, practical dive into reading charts** — from the anatomy of a single candle to a complete, repeatable framework for finding high-quality trades. It's the "how do I actually decide where to enter, stop, and target?" volume.

> ⚠️ **Set your expectations correctly.** Technical analysis is **not** a crystal ball. No pattern, indicator, or Fibonacci level "predicts" the future — they describe **probabilities and context**. TA works because it helps you (1) read what price is doing, (2) find repeatable, favorable locations to trade, and (3) define your risk precisely. It only makes money when combined with the **risk management of [Volume 1](../README.md)** and the **discipline of [Volume 2](../vol-2-top-traders/README.md)**. A beautiful chart read with no stop-loss still blows up an account.

This is **Volume 3**. It assumes you already know the basics from **[Volume 1 — Foundations](../README.md)** (candles, pips, support/resistance, indicators at a glance). Here we go far deeper.

## The core philosophy of this volume

```
1. STRUCTURE FIRST.   Read the trend & market structure before anything else.
2. LEVELS SECOND.     Mark where supply/demand and S/R actually are.
3. SIGNAL THIRD.      Wait for a candlestick / pattern trigger AT a level.
4. CONFLUENCE WINS.   The best trades stack multiple independent reasons.
5. INDICATORS CONFIRM, they don't lead. Price is king; indicators are advisors.
6. RISK ALWAYS.       Every read becomes a trade only with a defined stop & target.
```

> 🔑 **The golden rule of charting:** trade *what you see, not what you think.* Read the chart objectively, take only high-confluence setups at meaningful levels, and let risk management turn a good read into a good trade.
